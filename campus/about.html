Campus Ambassador

WORK OF A CAMPUS AMBASSADOR

“As a Campus Ambassador, you are expected to act as a link between the fest and your college“

Publicize

Ensure online publicity of Oneiros’19 through social media, in your city and especially in your college.

Communicate

You will be responsible for managing Oneiros, Manipal University Jaipur’s presence in your college.

Lead

Select CA’s will be given the opportunity to lead their college contingent during Oneiros.

INCENTIVES

Prizes such as headphones, mobiles and a lot more for the top performing Campus Ambassadors on the basis of performance.


Bi-weekly and monthly incentives plus prizes through the CA of month program.


The coveted Campus Ambassador certificate authenticating their work for Oneiros’19, Manipal University.


Letters of recommendation for exceptional performance.

LEARNING OPPORTUNITIES

A virtue tour of Oneiros’19 allowing you to interact with people managing the various departments of Oneiros’19 and get a first-hand idea of how different facets of one of the largest cultural fest in are managed.


Club workshops: We have 10 clubs, some of them include music, dance, literary, quizzing, etc. which has forged a name for them in the college circuit. We plan to conduct introductory workshops involving these clubs, where the participants will be given a flavor of that particular field.


The coveted Campus Ambassador certificate authenticating their work for Oneiros’19, Manipal University.
